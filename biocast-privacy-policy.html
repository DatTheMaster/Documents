Privacy Policy

BioCast is a wellness tool, not a medical device. The readiness scores, summaries, and recommendations provided by this app are intended for general wellness and self-awareness purposes only. They do not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Do not use BioCast as a substitute for professional medical judgment. If you have health concerns, consult a qualified healthcare provider.

Who We Are

BioCast is an independent, solo-operated wellness application. There is no corporation, venture-backed team, or data broker behind this product — just one developer building a tool intended to be genuinely useful without exploiting your data.

For privacy questions or data requests, contact: datthemaster@gmail.com

What Data We Collect

BioCast reads health and fitness data from Apple HealthKit on your device. Specifically, we access:

  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Resting heart rate (RHR)
  • Sleep sessions including duration, efficiency, and sleep stage data
  • Active energy burned
  • Exercise minutes and workout data

In addition to health data, we store:

  • A unique user identifier generated automatically on first launch
  • Your first name (optional, used for personalization)
  • Your time zone (used to align daily reports to your local day)
  • Push notification token (if you grant notification permission)
  • Your account tier (free or premium)

We do not collect your legal name, government ID, financial information, location, contacts, photos, or any data outside of what is listed above.

How We Store Your Data

Your health data is transmitted over HTTPS to our backend API and stored in a database hosted by Supabase, located in the United States (Oregon region). Data is stored under your anonymous user ID and is protected by row-level security — no other user can access your records.

Our API is hosted on Fly.io in the Chicago, IL region. Communication between the mobile app and API uses JWT-authenticated HTTPS.

Your health data never leaves secure, encrypted infrastructure. We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or third-party tracking of any kind inside the BioCast app.

AI-Generated Content

Powered by AI

BioCast uses artificial intelligence to generate personalized wellness summaries, readiness headlines, and daily recommendations. We believe you deserve to know exactly how this works.

Your anonymized biometric data — specifically aggregated readiness score, HRV trend, sleep quality, and strain level — is sent to Anthropic's Claude API (currently the claude-haiku-4-5-20251001 model) to generate your daily summary text. This processing happens server-side through our nightly pipeline; the Claude API call is never made from your device.

Data sent to Anthropic contains:

  • Numeric scores and signal values (e.g., readiness: 72, HRV trend: declining)
  • Categorical labels (e.g., sleep quality: moderate, strain: high)
  • No names, email addresses, device identifiers, or personally identifying information

Anthropic's data handling is governed by their Privacy Policy. Per Anthropic's API usage terms, data submitted via the API is not used to train their models by default.

AI-generated content in BioCast is clearly framed as a wellness interpretation of your data, not a clinical assessment. The model can be wrong. Treat its output accordingly.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the BioCast service to you. Specifically:

  • To calculate your daily readiness score and wellness metrics
  • To build a rolling baseline that personalizes your scores over time
  • To generate your daily AI wellness summary
  • To deliver your morning push notification
  • To display your history within the app

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except as described in this policy (Supabase for storage, Anthropic for AI generation, Fly.io for API hosting, Apple for push delivery).

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Daily forecast records and biomarker history are retained to support trend analysis and baseline personalization — this is core to how the app functions.

You may request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing datthemaster@gmail.com. Deletion requests will be processed within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

BioCast is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data to BioCast, please contact us immediately.

Apple HealthKit

BioCast accesses Apple HealthKit data solely to provide features within the app. HealthKit data will not be used for advertising, shared with data brokers, or used for any purpose other than delivering the BioCast service as described in this policy. This is consistent with Apple's HealthKit developer guidelines.
